#### Always Prepared

**Cost:** 10 EXP  
**Requirements:** Max Intelligence 11+, Trained in Sleight  
**Genres:** Any

You may find any mundane item amongst your belongings as though you have always had it, but you must pay for the item normally. The item must be something you can easily carry and conceal, without causing you to become encumbered. If you don't have access to your belongings, then you can't use this talent. Any items paid for with this ability cost 10% less. This ability can be used a total number of times per rest based on its advancement.

Both the material cost of that item, and the focus cost, are determined by the item being created. All items created by this ability lose their effects if not used before a week has passed since their creation. Items can only contain one synthesized magic effect at a time. Using this ability on an item that already contains a synthesized magic effect will replace the old one.

The advancement of this talent determines what types of items can be created.

#### Chaotic Magic

**Cost:** 10 EXP  
**Requirements:** Max Resolve 11+, Can cast spells  
**Genres:** Fantasy

Before casting any spell you may choose to invoke the power of chaos, forcibly altering your spell. If you do, first choose your spell then roll r10 and use the following table to determine how it is altered. You may then make any choices for that spell.

You must then attempt to cast the spell after rolling for the random effect. If you are unable to even begin casting the spell, considered it a failed spell that costs 1 focus, and end your turn (if during an encounter and it is your turn).

##### Table: Chaotic Magic Modifiers
| Roll | Modifier |
|:-:|:-|
| 10 | You must cast an additional spell at the same time (with the same or lower action cost) |
| 9 | You must cast a different spell of the same tier or one tier higher, but with a difficulty of the currently chosen spell |
| 8 | +2 to your spellcasting roll |
| 7 | The spell is considered one success category higher |
| 6 | The spell costs half its focus cost, rounded up (after any adjustments) |
| 5 | The spell is cast at one action speed faster (extended spells ignore this effect) |
| 4 | The spell is cast normally |
| 3 | The spell deals damage to you instead of its focus cost |
| 2 | -2 to your spellcasting roll |
| 1 | You must cast a different spell of the same tier or one tier lower, but with a difficulty of the currently chosen spell |

#### Esoteric Will

**Cost:** 10 EXP  
**Requirements:** Max Resolve 11+, Can use powers  
**Genres:** Any

You may pay focus costs to use powers putting your focus into the negative. If you do, make a willpower check versus a difficulty equal to double your negative focus. Make this check after using the power in this way (regardless of degree of success).

***Critical:*** Nothing bad happens.  
***Success:*** You lose 1 health.  
***Graze:*** You lose health equal to half that ability's focus cost (rounded up), and cannot use the same power until you take a break.  
***Fail:*** You lose health equal to that ability's focus cost, and cannot use any powers until you take a rest.

#### Metapowers

**Cost:** 10 EXP  
**Requirements:** Max Intelligence 13+ or Max Resolve 13+, Can use at least 5 powers  
**Genres:** Any

When you acquire this talent you will choose a metapower category from the table below. This talent can be taken multiple times (once for each category and with the usual cost increase).

Before using any power, you may choose to modify it using any metapower talents you have acquired. When you do, you will increase or decrease its focus cost for each use of any metapower modifier. Powers cannot be adjusted by metapowers that change features they do not have. Similarly, powers cannot be adjusted simultaneously by metapowers that have opposite modifiers, like widened and reduced, or channelled and quickened.

For each power that you use, you can modify it a number of times equal to the total number of different metapower talents that you have. However, each individual metapower can only be applied half this many times each in this way (rounded up). For example: If you have 5 different metapowers, each one can individually be applied up to 3 times, but the total number of applications is limited to 5.

##### Table: Metapowers
| Metapower | Focus Cost Adjustment | Modifier |
|:-|:-:|:-|
| Bloodied | -1 | The power causes you to lose 1 health |
| Channelled | -1 | The power is used with one half more action cost (rounded up) (when you stack this metapower it increases from one half more to double, to tripled, and so on) |
| Empowered | +1 | The power gets a +2 bonus to any willpower rolls |
| Fickle | - | The power has its duration halved (when you stack this metapower it decreases from halved to thirded, to quartered, and so on) |
| Heightened | +1 | The power gets +3 to its difficulty to be resisted |
| Intensified | +1 | The power inflicts effects to a degree of one higher |
| Persistent | +1 | The power has its duration doubled (when you stack this metapower it increases from doubled to tripled, to quadrupled, and so on) |
| Quickened | +1 | The power is used with its action cost reduced by half (rounded down) (when you stack this metapower it decreases from half to a third, to a quarter, and so on) |
| Reaching | +1 | The power has its range doubled, or increased to 3m if it had a range of only touch (when you stack this metapower it increases from doubled to tripled, to quadrupled, and so on) |
| Reduced | - | The power has its area or radius halved (when you stack this metapower it decreases from halved to thirded, to quartered, and so on) |
| Shortened | - | The power has its range halved (when you stack this metapower it decreases from halved to thirded, to quartered, and so on) |
| Triggered | +1 | The power is used, but its effects only occur when a specified trigger<sup>1</sup> is met (holding this trigger requires concentration, lasting 24 hours, until the concentration is lost or the spell is triggered) |
| Widened | +2 | The power has its area or radius doubled (when you stack this metapower it increases from doubled to tripled, to quadrupled, and so on) |

<sup>1</sup> For triggered metapowers any condition is valid so long as it is knowable by the user or provided by the context of the power.

#### Prepared Defense

**Cost:** 10 EXP  
**Requirements:** Max Dexterity 11+ or Max Resolve 11+, Trained in any combat skill  
**Genres:** Any

The first defensive maneuver reaction that you use each round is a free reaction, and you may use each defensive maneuver twice each round instead of only once.

#### Quick Step

**Cost:** 10 EXP  
**Requirements:** Max Dexterity 11+, Trained in Agility  
**Genres:** Any

Your speed is increased by 2m, and you can move up to a 2m (from 1m) during a Move action as a free action.

#### Rage

**Cost:** 10 EXP  
**Requirements:** Max Strength 11+, Max Resolve 11+, Trained in any combat skill  
**Genres:** Any

You gain an ability allowing you to fly into a rage as a free action or reaction when certain conditions are met, giving you a temporary bonus to both strength and damage reduction. This damage reduction bonus is halved while wearing body armour (rounded up). These bonuses vary depending on the advancement of this talent. Additionally, while in a rage your total damage reduction applies even when you are critically hit (instead of zero damage reduction).

Before you can enter a rage you must be reduced to an amount of endurance less than or equal to half of your maximum, or if you have anay amount of health less than your maximum.

Being in a rage requires that you maintain concentration on the rage. During any of your turns (except during a turn that you activate rage) you may choose to end the rage effect as a free action. At the end of each turn that the rage persists (except for turn the it is activated) you lose 1 stamina or take 1 damage (this damage cannot be prevented in any way). Rage cannot be interrupted or lost except by becoming unconscious or manually ending the rage.

#### Relentless Offense

**Cost:** 10 EXP  
**Requirements:** Max Strength 11+ or Max Dexterity 11+, Trained in any combat skill  
**Genres:** Any

The first offensive maneuver that you use each round costs one less stamina, and you may use each offensive maneuver twice each round instead of only once.

#### Second Wind

**Cost:** 10 EXP  
**Requirements:** Max Dexterity 11+, Max Resolve 11+, Trained in Athletics  
**Genres:** Any

As an action you may recover stamina equal to half of your maximum (rounded up). Additionally, any maneuvers you use during the same turn cost zero stamina. This ability can be used only once per rest.

#### Sprinting

**Cost:** 10 EXP  
**Requirements:** Max Strength 11+, Max Dexterity 11+, Trained in Athletics  
**Genres:** Any

Your speed is increased by 2m, and you may spend 1 stamina on your first move action each turn to move double your speed.

---

#### Steady Mind

**Cost:** 10 EXP  
**Requirements:** Max Intelligence 11+, Max Resolve 11+, Can use powers  
**Genres:** Any

When you become unconscious or stunned, instead of immediately losing concentration on powers, you may make a willpower check against the difficulty to use that power. If successful, the concentration is maintained.

Against any other effects that would cause you to lose concentration, gain advantage to your resist check to maintain concentration.

---

#### Supernatural Resistance

**Cost:** 10 EXP  
**Requirements:** Max Strength 11+, Max Resolve 13+  
**Genres:** Any

Whenever a power that requires concentration is applying an effect to you, every round you can make an additional check to resist it. Additionally, you are always aware of the direction that a power is coming from as it applies to you.

#### Tactician

**Requirements:** Max Intelligence 11+, Trained in any combat skill  
**Genres:** Any

You may spend focus instead of stamina to perform tactical maneuvers. This talent can only be used to perform learned maneuvers, and not improvised maneuvers.

#### Wild Intuition

**Cost:** 10 EXP

**Requirements:** Max Intelligence 11+ or Max Resolve 11+, Trained in Perception  
**Genres:** Any

Your ability to notice and remember details has expanded your natural perceptions. You can always tell the time of day, what direction is north, and where the nearest source of running water is. These abilities persist even in conditions where they would otherwise not be possible.
